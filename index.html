Invisible Rush – Account and Data Deletion

Invisible Rush is developed by Games Factory Dev.

The game itself does not require users to create an account in order to play.
However, if a user chooses to connect to the leaderboard feature using the "Connect" button, this will redirect them to create or use an existing GDevelop account.

How to delete my entire GDevelop account

To initiate a full account deletion, follow these steps:

  1. Open the GDevelop app (mobile app, desktop app or the web app).
  2. Login with your profile, if it's not already done.
  3. Open your profile page by clicking on your username in the GDevelop homepage.
  4. In your Profile, click Edit my profile.
  5. At the bottom, click Delete my account.

Validate your choice after clicking on Delete my account. Your account will be removed and inaccessible soon after.

Data that will be deleted:
- Leaderboard-related data (such as scores and user identifiers)
- GDevelop account data associated with the leaderboard connection

Data retention:
- Data deletion is handled by GDevelop according to their privacy policy.
- Invisible Rush does not store or retain any personal user data on its own servers.
